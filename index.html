About Us

Warm Greetings from Government College of Engineering, Dharmapuri

We are eternally Grateful to invite you to FETRA 2K23. The Event is to be Presented by Electronics and Communication Department of Government college of Engineering ,Dharmapuri. FETRA 2K23 is a National level Symposium to be held on 17th of April it contains a sequence of Technical Event to ehance Knowledge and other amusement activities to make the event Noteworthy

This Symposium encloses technical events like

  • Paper Presentation
  • Project expo
  • Technical Quiz
  • Code Debugging
  • Gaming

It is entirely organised by a team of dedicated students, to reveal the inner self of students, from ECE. The event authorize the participants to expose their Knowledge in Forthcoming Technology. It doesn't end there, we've got surprising rewards for the winners.

Register now and join us to interact with the technical ideas and build your knowledge with pleasure
